# BU1 Blog Keyword Gap Analysis

Date: 2026-04-10

## Scope

This analysis combines:

- current BU1 blog coverage inferred from article titles in `BU1_translations/*.csv`
- Czech search phrasing observed in public search results
- BU1 business fit for goalkeepers, youth football, and goalkeeper gear

This is a market-informed content gap analysis.

It is **not** a first-party keyword analysis from Google Search Console or GA4. When those data sources are connected, the backlog should be re-ranked.

## Current Coverage Summary

Observed from 122 current article titles:

- strong presence of promotional/news content
- strong presence of interviews, transfer news, and broad football topics
- only moderate evergreen goalkeeper coverage
- very weak coverage of focused goalkeeper training queries and glove-care education

Rough signal from title clustering:

- news/promo: about 28 titles
- interviews/profiles: about 11 titles
- goalkeeper evergreen: about 31 titles
- generic football topics: about 24 titles

Conclusion:

- several commercially useful evergreen clusters are missing entirely from titles

## Topics Missing Entirely From Current Titles

No clear title-level coverage was found for:

- goalkeeper reaction training / postřeh
- pre-match goalkeeper warm-up / rozcvička
- goalkeeper distribution / hra nohama, rozehrávka
- catching crosses / centry, vysoké míče
- 1v1 situations / samostatný nájezd
- diving technique / pádová technika
- goalkeeper communication with defenders
- glove size selection
- glove washing and care
- goalkeeper positioning / postavení brankáře

## Topics I Would Not Prioritize Right Now

- more transfer roundups
- more broad football history pieces
- more generic football culture topics
- more profile/interview content unless tied to a strong search or campaign objective

Reason:

BU1’s moat is goalkeeper expertise plus product trust. The blog currently spends too much surface area outside that moat.
